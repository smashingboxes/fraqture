Fraqture is an experimental platform that blends art with technology, exploring the parallels between glitch art and graffiti.

Conceived as an installation for the Durham Storefront Project and in collaboration with The Carrack, digital product agency Smashing Boxes constructed Fraqture with a large monitor, Arduino board and 2 handmade panels containing over 500 LEDs.

The glitch code is open source and written in Clojure and C. The featured artwork is curated by 21c and The Carrack.

"We wanted to create something for the artist and technology communities of Durham"
